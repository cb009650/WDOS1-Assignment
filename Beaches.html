Sri Lanka is known as the recommended go to beach destination Worldwide, and has continued to hold that reptation for many years. Made to perfection with its soft sands, clear blue skies and welcoming locals, the beaches of Sri Lanka will be the perfect start to your holiday as you begin to surround yourself with Sri Lankan culture. Therefore the beautiful nation humbly invites you to come visit the golden shores and take a dip in the brilliant blue waters. Below are a few well known beaches in Sri Lanka which are perfect for tourists.

Arugam Bay

A-bay is known as a surfer's paradise! And for those who are thrill seekers who love to go surfing. The perfect place for that is definitely Arugam Bay. Located in the east coast of Sri Lanka Arugam Bay is regarded as one of the top 10 places in the world to go surfing, and the best time to ride the waves will be between May and September. The Elephant Rock is one of the best places to gaze at ravishing sunsets and will never fail to amaze you! Don't forget to take a camera along with you as this will be a moment you would want to capture and look back at.

All sorts of transport are available to get to Arugam Bay such as buses and trains. Tuks are the best way to drive around the town while the wind hits you. It's also an amazing spot to kick back and relax, while all your troubles disappear with the wind. Reading books in hammocks in the evening while sipping a mouthwatering cocktail is an all-time favorite for tourists. Don't leave Sri Lanka without checking out this spectacular beach. You won't regret spending a few nights here and having the best time of your life!

Unawatuna beach

Unawatuna beach also known as the “banana shaped” beach in located in the Galle district and has a beautiful shade of clear blue water and palm trees. It is also one of the most famous beaches in Sri Lanka and is best visited during the months December to April. Tragically Unawatuna was ruined by the tsunami in 2004 but was rebuilt again to its former glory by hardworking citizens.

Unawatuna Centre is an amazing place to go shopping with gorgeous jewelry and souvenirs to buy and delicious food to eat which will make your mouth water. For a quieter and more peaceful vibe, check out the Jungle Beach or Secret Beach Mirissa. For someone who loves coral reefs, palm trees, sun kissed sand and crystal-clear water, a few days here would make you never want to leave!

Bentota beach

The Bentota beach, situated in the southwest coast of Sri Lanka, and is best during the months October and April is known for its scenic view which is relished by many tourists from around the world. The large variety of water sports that are available, such as jet-skiing, wind surfing, snorkeling, fishing, dolphin watching and parasailing in the cold clear water will make your holiday in Sri Lanka an extraordinary, thrilling and an unforgettable one!

Your vacation is not complete until you attend an amusing Bentota beach party with clamorous music and dancing along with the scrumptious drinks that will not let you down. However, a more nonchalant aura is there away from the clubs and parties with a relaxing breeze surrounding you. The restaurants in Bentota are known for its delicious seafood dishes which you won't be able to find anywhere else and will leave you wanting more. Benota beach is definitely a place that needs to be on your bucket list!
